Five prototypes to pick from

Direct response to your 5 marching orders after the TapNow + Freepik studies. Each section below is a clickable mock plus the rationale. Decide YES / REFINE / NO at the bottom of each, then I build it.

What we learned

  • TapNow vocab (Node / Wire / Group / Canvas) is literally ours — they validated the design class.
  • Freepik DeepImagine puts a giant prompt + mode toggle + model chips on the home screen. Composer-first wins.
  • Both ship live interactive demos on the marketing site — screenshots don't sell.
  • Pricing pattern: cheap entry, slider within tier, credits never expire, top-up bonus at higher tier.

What ArchHub keeps that they can't

  • Real Revit / AutoCAD / 3ds Max / Rhino / Blender hosts.
  • Local-first, offline, BYO key.
  • Speckle wires — content-addressed, version-immutable, undo via previous Version.
  • Vertical depth in AEC — they're general creative, we're architects-and-engineers-specific.
