Verdict

Proof-scale, not production-scale — yet. 396 rows / ~32k tokens is far too small to train a model from scratch (that needs millions of tokens). But it's enough to fine-tune a small adapter (LoRA) on a base model as a working proof — a few hundred good, provenance-tagged examples measurably shift behaviour. The path to a real model is the brain growing: more projects, more devices, and eventually the community scope (now 0) pooling privacy-protected facts across users. That's the north-star loop already wired in the app.
